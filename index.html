How to Play Twain Says

Rearrange the tiles to reveal a Mark Twain quotation, reading from left to right, top to bottom. You can rearrange the tiles by sliding them vertically, but not horizontally.

There are empty cells at the top of the gameboard. You can use them to temporarily hold tiles while you rearrange other tiles on the game board.

Any empty cell that is not darkened can hold a tile. Darkened cells are the spaces between words.

Tips

The end of a row is not necessarily the end of a word. Words often—but not always—wrap from one row to the next.

There are no tiles for punctuation, like commas and apostrophes. You would spell "can't," for example, like this:

C
A
N
T
